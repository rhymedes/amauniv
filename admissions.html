Admissions & Contact Us

Ready to embark on your academic journey with AMA University? Find out how to apply, understand our admission process, and easily get in touch with our dedicated team.

How to Apply

Admission Requirements

  • Original High School Report Card (Form 138)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • Original Birth Certificate (PSA copy)
  • Two (2) pieces of 2x2 ID Pictures
  • Photocopy of any valid ID (for guardian/parent)
  • For transferees: Official Transcript of Records and Honorable Dismissal

*Please note that specific requirements may vary per program and level of study. We highly recommend contacting the admissions office for the most accurate and up-to-date details.

Admission Process

  1. Submit all complete admission requirements to the AMA University Admissions Office.
  2. Take the required entrance examination (if applicable for your chosen program).
  3. Attend a scheduled interview with the Dean or relevant Department Head.
  4. Upon successful evaluation, you will receive your Official Notice of Acceptance.
  5. Proceed to the final enrollment process to secure your slot.

For online application procedures or any specific inquiries, please utilize the contact form below or reach out to us directly via phone.
